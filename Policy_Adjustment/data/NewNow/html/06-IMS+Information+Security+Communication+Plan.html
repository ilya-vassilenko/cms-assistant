Purpose, scope, and target audience

Purpose: This policy governs data protection– and information security–related communications within AlpineAI.

Scope: This policy applies to all communication channels and any communication regarding information security or data protection.

Target audience: Defined in the Applicability Matrix.

Relevant Documents

  • Customer Contacts for Information Security and Data Protection

  • Inventory of Subprocessor Notifications

Related Standards & Procedures

  • Section “7.4 Communication” of the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard.

Internal Communication

Availability of policies. Information security and data protection policies shall be available to employees on Confluence.

Acknowledgements. Major versions of policies that require formal employee acknowledgement shall be hosted in Vanta, and employee approvals shall be recorded.

Key decisions. Important information security and data protection decisions relevant to the entire team shall be communicated in monthly meetings.

Ad-hoc updates. Ad hoc decisions and relevant information shall be communicated directly (peer-to-peer or in groups via email or Slack).

Communication with Customers

Contacts in agreements. Data Protection Agreements shall include the customer’s contact details for data protection and information security matters. If a dedicated contact is not specified, the main contractual contact shall be used.

Inventory of Customer Contacts. Alpine AI shall maintain a list of contacts for all customers to streamline communications required for subprocessor change notifications, incident reporting and other relevant matters. Key Account Management shall ensure that contact details remain up to date.
Note: See section “Related Documents” for the link to the Inventory of Customer Contacts.

Subprocessor notifications. Notifications regarding changes to subprocessors shall be communicated in writing. The notification and the recipient list shall be stored in SharePoint for traceability.
Note: See section “Related Documents” for the link to the Inventory of Subprocessor Notifications.

Communication with Authorities

  • Role as processor. With regard to customer data, Alpine AI acts as a processor; the primary communication obligations rest with its customers as controllers.

  • Coordination with customers. If an authority contacts Alpine AI, the company shall communicate only in collaboration with the customer and with the customer’s approval.

  • Incident reporting. Major incidents shall be reported to authorities where there is a legal requirement to do so.
