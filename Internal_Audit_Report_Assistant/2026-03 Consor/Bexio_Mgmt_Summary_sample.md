# Management Summary

Arxio AG conducted an internal ISO/IEC 27001:2022 audit at bexio AG. bexio has recently procured a
Vanta license to support the management of its ISMS; the ISMS-relevant policies
have been uploaded to Vanta and approved by management, and supporting evidence
artifacts have been provided for a range of controls. The audit was performed
through a combination of remote document and evidence review (based on the
information maintained in Vanta) as well as remote and on-site interviews and
validation sessions.

bexio has implemented a variety of security measures and operational processes over an extended
period and has recently formalized these practices within a structured ISMS. No
major nonconformities were identified that would materially impair information
security or jeopardize ISO/IEC 27001 certification readiness. The
nonconformities observed are largely attributable to the recent introduction of
Vanta and the associated transition to more formalized ISMS governance, and are
expected to be remediated within a short timeframe. In addition, several
improvement recommendations were discussed with the relevant stakeholders
during the audit.

This report summarizes, for the sampled control set, (i) which governance documents address
specific ISO/IEC 27001 requirements and controls, (ii) how the controls are
implemented in practice, (iii) the identified nonconformities, and (iv) the
agreed recommendations for further strengthening the ISMS. The audit scope
covered approximately one third of the ISO/IEC 27001 control set, selected
based on business relevance, to enable full coverage of the ISO standard over a
three-year audit cycle.

## Beschreibung der Firma

bexio AG (Alte Jonastrasse 24, 8640 Rapperswil, Switzerland; UID:
CHE-322.646.985) is a Swiss software company providing a cloud-based business
administration platform for small businesses, self-employed professionals and
start-ups, with a focus on simplifying day-to-day back-office processes (e.g.,
customer/contact management, quoting/offers, invoicing, reminders, and
integrated e-banking).

The company traces its roots to the “easySYS” initiative that was
subsequently rebranded to bexio; the public company timeline indicates early
product use in 2012, and an official founding as “easySYS” in 2013, followed by
the rebrand to bexio in 2015. In 2018, bexio was acquired by Mobiliar and
operates as an independent subsidiary. The headquarter is located in Rapperswil
with an additional location in Vevey.

## Produktinformation

bexio positions its solution as an all-in-one, web-based platform for
SME administration. Core capabilities include:

- **Business administration modules:** Contact management, preparation of offers/quotes, invoicing with automated reminders, and e-banking integration to support payment processing and reconciliation workflows.
- **Accounting and payroll:** bexio provides
  accounting functionality designed to automate bookkeeping activities; it also
  offers payroll accounting as a product component, including a dedicated
  offering for Swiss payroll.
- **Mobile enablement:** the bexio Go
  mobile app (iOS/Android) supports, among other functions, receipt/document
  capture and processing (including QR invoice scanning) and mobile time tracking
  (package-dependent).
- **Customer base:** On the day of the audit,
  bexio registered the 100’000^th^ paying customer.
- **Language availability:** The software is available
  in German, French, Italian and English.
- **Primary market:** The platform is
  explicitly tailored to Swiss SMEs and Swiss administrative requirements (e.g.,
  positioning, banking connectivity, trustee collaboration, and local language
  coverage).
- **Ecosystem**: fiduciaries (trustees), partners, integrations.
- **Fiduciary collaboration model:** bexio
  supports collaboration between SMEs and fiduciaries (trustees) via account
  access/invitations and a dedicated trustee proposition. It also advertises a
  network of certified fiduciary partners (over 1,300 according to bexio’s
  website) and is discoverable via a fiduciary directory.
- **Marketplace and add-ons:** bexio operates an
  app marketplace to complement the core platform with additional business
  functions and third-party integrations.
- **API / integration capabilities:**
  bexio provides a REST API for integrating external applications; it also states
  that it does not restrict API access for building integrations (with partner
  collaboration options for businesses built around the API).
- **Data residency statement (customer-facing).** bexio’s public-facing materials state that customer data is stored
  securely in Switzerland, including references to (certified) Swiss data centers
  in the trustee-focused description.
