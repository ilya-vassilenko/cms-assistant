2 Introduction

2.1 Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to safeguard employees and Frontify.

﻿

﻿

2.2 Scope

  • All Frontify employees

  • External third-party users (including but not limited to agencies, marketing service providers, etc.)

﻿

﻿

2.3 Principle

This policy ensures clear procedures for employee onboarding and offboarding, including access revocation processes, to mitigate potential risks associated with staff changes and transitions within the organization.

3 Human Resources Security Policy

3.1 Candidate and Background Screening

The candidate screening process is a shared responsibility between the People Success teams, namely the Talent Acquisition team as well as the People Support team.


﻿

3.2 Recruitment Screening

The recruitment screening shall be tailored to the specified needs of the respective role. Whenever a new position is to be filled, the role and its criteria shall be documented and published where feasible. Appropriate criteria such as needed experience level, needed skill and company fit shall be taken into account during recruitment.


﻿

3.3 Non-Conformance with Background Screening

If a candidate fails to meet conformance with criminal records and debt collection requirements, a case-by-case decision shall be taken by applicable parties (VP People, Team Lead, Information Security Office, and Talent Acquisition Lead) by taking into account equality and the avoidance of discrimination.

﻿

﻿

3.4 Terms & Conditions

Employment terms & conditions of a respective role shall be defined in the employment contract which must be signed by Frontify candidates before the start. A list of applicable employment laws, as well as legally required documents, are set forth in the FISMS-007A List of Legal, Regulatory and Contractual Obligations.

﻿

All employment contracts including annexes must be stored in Personio and shall be accessible to employees at all times.

﻿

﻿

3.5 Onboarding

Onboardings shall be initiated by the People Support team and must be triaged between different departments.

﻿

﻿

3.6 Employee Responsibilities for Information Security

In line with Frontify's Information Security Management System, the CISO , the Information Security Office or the Team Lead, shall make employees aware of the responsibilities they uphold during their work relationship by triggering the "statement of acceptance" process within 30 days after the employee's start at Frontify.

﻿

﻿

3.7 Disciplinary Process

If employees fail to conform with information security responsibilities or seriously violate Information Security standards, the CISO shall assess the severity of such a violation and shall inform the senior management (preferably VP of People). If necessary, the senior management shall invoke an appropriate disciplinary process which may include a warning up to contract termination.


﻿

3.8 Change of Department

When an employee changes to another department, a department change workflow shall be triggered by the People Support Team.

﻿

﻿

3.9 General Offboardings

In case of separation between an employee and the company, the employee or the team lead shall inform the People Support team which shall then trigger the offboarding workflow and automatically inform the Information Security Office and IT Operations Team via email. Details in the email shall include:

  • Name of the offboardee

  • Last working day


﻿3.10 Asset Offboarding

The CISO or the Information Security Office shall, after the leave is made official to the respective team and, if applicable, to the whole company, coordinate and initiate the asset offbaording via the IT Operations team.


﻿

3.11 Regular Reviews of Offboardings

Both the People Support offboarding workflow, as well as the asset access offboarding workflow, shall be subject to regular reviews of completeness.

﻿

﻿

3.12 Confirmation of Information Asset Returns

As described in the FISMS-020 Acceptable Use Policy employees must return all information assets to the company upon termination of the employment contract or other contract on the basis of which equipment, software or information in electronic or paper form was provided or created.
﻿

The IT Operations team may copy and remove all files related to Frontify on Employee-Owned Devices.

﻿

Upon return of the information assets described, the People Support team shall issue a Confirmation of Asset Returns via email and shall store the confirmation date in Personio.

﻿

﻿

3.13 Ongoing Responsibilities after the Termination of Employment

Team Leads, as well as the People Support team, shall advise the leaving employee of the following responsibilities:

  • Responsibilities as described in the Confidentiality Agreement Clause

  • Responsibilities as described in the Anti-Competition Agreement Clause

  • Any other applicable responsibilities

The advisory shall be included in the confirmation of assets email on the employee's last contractually agreed day.

4 Policy Compliance

4.1 Compliance Measurement

The Information Security Office will verify compliance to this policy through various methods, including but not limited to, business tool reports, internal and external audits, and feedback to the policy owner.

﻿

﻿

4.2 Exceptions

Any exception to the policy must be approved and recorded by the Information Security Office in advance and reported to the CISO.

﻿

﻿

4.3 Non-Compliance

An employee found to have violated the Frontify Information Security Management System may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

﻿

﻿

4.4 Continual Improvement

The policy is updated and reviewed as part of the continual improvement process.
